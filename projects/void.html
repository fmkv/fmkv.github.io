PORTFOLIO | voiding the void: residence

UC Berkeley Introduction to Design Studio, Fall 2020

Voiding the Void is an exploration the formal logic derived from David Adjaye's Elektra House. The interlocking voids forming double negative spaces in the precedent study are carried forth and applied to the dwelling, the site and the pool house program.
