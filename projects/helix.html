PORTFOLIO | double helix: market place

UC Berkeley Introduction to Architecture Studio, Spring 2021

This project is an exploration of the ways rectilinear spaces respond to the integration of circles and curves. It arose from a merging of the Amsterdam exchange and the helical ramp circulation system. This community marketspace is integrated into the Dogpatch neighborhood of San Francisco.
