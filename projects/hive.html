PORTFOLIO | the hive: james r. boyce housing studio

Collaborated with: Sam Miller & Lin Hu (M.Arch), Louisa Bukiet & Obina Uwakah (finance)
UC Berkeley Boyce Housing Studio, Spring 2022

The Hive is a 250 unit affordable housing complex developed in excess state lands in Santa Cruz, CA. The design responds to the site's unique shape, the adjoining floodplain and the community's unique needs.

window design for the hive

Collaborated with: Sam Miller & Lin Hu (M.Arch), Louisa Bukiet & Obina Uwakah (finance)
UC Berkeley Ulrich's Window, Spring 2022

The Hive's single loaded corridor design allowed units to have windows on both ends, allowing for better daylighting and natural ventilation. This window design faces the exterior corridors and while presrving the privacy of the residents.
